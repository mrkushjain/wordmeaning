Idea:
My mother needed one word substitution (word meaning) for preparetion of her CTET exam.  I searched on the net and found a pdf and copied it to my samsung android smartphone. But there was a huge spacing betweeen the meaning and the word which made it difficult to read on the phone. So I made this program to reduce the spacing

Functions
 Reads a textfile line by line.  It extracts the last word of the line and identifies it as the word and the rest of the line as its meaning. It then formats the output of the line in the form of a row of a table containing two columns, one for the word and other for the meaning
